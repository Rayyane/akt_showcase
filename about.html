About us

Akij Tableware offers a range of tableware that adheres to the highest standards of quality and design. Each piece has been carefully crafted to exude finesse and refinement, and is designed to elevate the dining experience. With imperial-inspired designs, our collection imbues an air of elegance to everyday dining. Make every meal an occasion to remember with the opulence of Akij Tableware. At Akij Tableware, we place a strong emphasis on creativity and originality, which is evident in the unique attributes and styles of our products. As a brand under the umbrella of Akij Ceramics Limited, a subsidiary of the esteemed Akij Bashir Group, one of the most prominent business conglomerates in Bangladesh, we uphold the Group's reputation for excellence in all aspects of our operations



Why We are Unique

Akij Tableware is known for its exceptional range of tableware that is distinguished by its variety of shapes, styles, and colors.

The products of Akij Tableware are crafted with a strong glaze finish that imparts durability and a sleek, polished appearance. The collections are stackable, making them easy to store and transport.

Additionally, they are vitrified and stain-resistant, making them easy to clean and maintain. These tableware are also built to withstand different temperatures, they are freezer safe, heat and chill retention, oven safe, microwave safe, dishwasher safe, thermal shock resistant and chip resistant.

All these features make Akij Tableware truly unique and suitable for any kitchen use, and make them stand out from other tableware brands in the market

Our Factory

Akij Tableware prides itself on a state-of-the-art factory located in Mymensingh, spanning a massive 13 acres or 566,280 square feet. The facility boasts a workforce of over 1,300 individuals, with a strong commitment to gender inclusion. Utilizing European technology, the factory has a production capacity of 2,40,000 pieces per 85 MT. Not only is the factory highly efficient and productive, but it also maintains strict adherence to environmental regulations, making it one of the most well-maintained and eco-friendly factories in all of Bangladesh.

Our capability

Akij Tableware is a renowned manufacturer of a diverse range of tableware that boasts of versatile shapes and an array of color options. The company's products are available in four distinct gloss finishes - regular, rustic, reactive, and dual tone. Utilizing state-of-the-art molding techniques, the company's creative craftsmen create products that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing.
The designs for the plates are the brainchild of the company's highly creative and imaginative designers. These designs are then meticulously printed on the plates, imbuing each product with an individualized and unique appeal.

Akij Tableware, under the umbrella of Akij Ceramics Limited, a subsidiary of the esteemed Akij Bashir Group, is a leader in innovation and individuality, delivering unique and original styles. Committed to quality and excellence, Akij Tableware strives to uphold the Group's reputation for distinction in Bangladesh.
